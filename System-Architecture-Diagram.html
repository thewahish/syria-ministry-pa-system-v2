üìã System Setup & Configuration Notes

  • Frequency Coordination: Program all 4 wireless mics + 2 IEMs on non-interfering frequencies (Sennheiser Wireless Systems Manager software recommended)
  • Antenna Placement: Mount antennas at front of stage for best line-of-sight coverage (100-150m range)
  • Line Array Rigging: Each array of 4√ó KLA12 weighs 56kg. Use KLA AF flying frames with 290-300mm box truss. Minimum truss load rating: 300kg per pick point
  • Wired Mic Placement: Best for fixed podium positions where speakers remain stationary. Eliminates 2 wireless channels from frequency coordination
  • Mixer Routing: Send separate monitor mixes to each IEM transmitter to customize what each performer hears
  • System Redundancy: With 4 wireless + 2 wired, you have flexibility to switch if wireless issues occur during event
  • Battery Management: Charge all batteries night before event. BA 70 batteries provide 8 hours runtime
  • Setup Time: Allow 2-3 hours for complete system setup and sound check with 2-3 person crew

  • DO NOT ORDER equipment until wireless frequencies are verified legal in Syria
  • Contact Syrian Telecommunications & Post Regulatory Authority (SY-TPRA)
  • Common legal bands: 518-566 MHz, 606-614 MHz (must confirm for Syria)
