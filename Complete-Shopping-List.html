Why NOT Full Mid-Range (6 Wireless)?

Ask yourself: "Do we really need 6 people with wireless mics simultaneously?"

  • Most events: 2-3 speakers at podiums (wired works great)
  • Plus 4 wireless for flexibility
  • Saves $3,230 with no practical limitation

Frequency Coordination Notes:

  • All wireless systems must be on compatible frequencies
  • Mid-Range Value Package: 4 wireless mics + 2 IEMs = 6 total wireless channels (easier!)
  • Mid-Range Package: 6 wireless mics + 2 IEMs = 8 total wireless channels
  • All Sennheiser packages: Easiest coordination (same brand)
  • Premium Package: All Shure = seamless integration
  • Budget Package: Shure BLX + PSM 300 = same manufacturer, good coordination

Syria-Specific Considerations:

  1. Wireless Frequencies: Verify legal UHF bands in Syria

- Typical: 518-566 MHz, 606-614 MHz

- May require telecommunications license

  1. Power: All equipment is 220V/50Hz compatible
  2. Spare Parts: Recommend 20% spare battery inventory
  3. Training: Include operator training for digital systems
