## Questions & Answers

### Q: Can we just add more speakers to the old 1000-person system later?
**A:** Not recommended. Better to design correctly from start:
- Matching speaker sets ensure tonal consistency
- Proper system design for 2000 people from beginning
- Avoid partial system upgrades (costly, inconsistent results)
- Get correct flying frames and rigging now

### Q: Do we really need 30A circuits?
**A:** Yes. Technical explanation:
- 16,000W √∑ 220V = 72.7A theoretical
- With power factor and headroom: ~30A per circuit √ó 2 circuits
- 20A circuits would trip breakers during loud passages
- Professional systems require adequate power overhead

### Q: Can we use 2 arrays of 6 elements instead of 3 arrays of 4?
**A:** Yes, both work:
- **2 arrays of 6 (L/R):** Simpler rigging, wider stereo image
- **3 arrays of 4 (L/C/R):** Better center coverage, easier to aim
- Recommend: Consult with installer based on venue shape

### Q: Why doesn't the wireless system need to be bigger?
**A:** Wireless capacity is about number of speakers, not audience size:
- 6 microphone channels serves 6 speakers (adequate for most events)
- Wireless range (100-150m) already covers 3000 sq m venue
- No additional wireless equipment needed
